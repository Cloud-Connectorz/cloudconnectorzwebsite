Culture and Team

The culture at Cloud Connectorz is based on 3 pillars:

  1. Personal attention: building up a team, focus on the human connection. Be there for each other and having fun together.
  2. Respect: The culture is one of respect and inclusion. We are open, flat and always helping each other and encourage everyone to bring their unique perspectives to the table.
  3. Cloud Native with a focus on modern application/platform development. Passionate about learning.

The team at Cloud Connectorz is composed of experienced, highly skilled developers and engineers who are committed to delivering quality cloud solution. Their expertise covers a wide range of technologies, including cloud development, and cloud engineering. Cloud Connectorz provides consulting services to help customers with the design and implementation of their goals. Meet the team.
